A little about us

W.Aquino Fireworks former A.R.E Fireworks was established in 1896 and managed by Mr. Ricardo Aquino that eventually handled and managed now by his grandson Mr. Winson Saturno Aquino.

With its factory located at 223 Brgy. Bigaa, Cabuyao Laguna. The company’s mission is to cater quality yet affordable outdoor and indoor fireworks display that leaves unforgettable and wonderful memories to the customers.

We accept consumer and display for all occasions using a computerized firing system, guaranteed safe and 100% in good condition. We already joined exhibits,shows and variety of pyromusical competitions and made it to the first place.

W.Aquino Fireworks won First Place in a Pyromusical competition in Enchanted Kingdom in the year 1996 and became their supplier from the year 1996-2000. We are currently the accredited Fireworks supplier of some Wedding Events place in Tagaytay and Calabarzon.

Catering fireworks display for all occasions such as Weddings, Feast day, Anniversaries, Birthdays, New Years Eve Countdown and others.

Outdoor Fireworks

Fireworks are a class of low explosive pyrotechnic devices used for aesthetic and entertainment purposes. They are most commonly used in fireworks displays (also called a fireworks show or pyrotechnics), combining a large number of devices in an outdoor setting.


Indoor Fireworks

Indoor fireworks are a type of pyrotechnic display that is designed to be used indoors. They are typically smaller and less powerful than traditional outdoor fireworks, and they use a different type of propellant that produces less smoke and noise..


Colored Smoke and Confetti Blaster

Colored smoke and confetti blasters are popular ways to announce a baby's gender in a fun and dramatic way. Colored smoke cannons release a cloud of blue or pink smoke, indicating the baby's gender, while confetti blasters shoot blue or pink confetti high into the air, creating a festive and exciting atmosphere. Both are designed to add an element of surprise and excitement to gender reveal celebrations, making them a memorable way to share the news with friends and family.
